Guru Randhawa

Lahore is a Punjabi Indi-pop single by Guru Randhawa, released by T-Series on 13 December 2017. It is one of the most-streamed songs in India. It has over 1 billion views on YouTube. It was recreated by Sachin-Jigar as Lagdi Lahore Di from the film Street Dancer 3D (2020), voiced by Guru Randhawa and Tulsi Kumar.
